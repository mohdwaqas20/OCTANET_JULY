    About The College


    PSIT College of Higher Education darted on the path of Higher Education in the year 2015 under the aegis of "Sahyog Jankalyan Samiti", whose members have wide experience in the field of education and industry. The college is affiliated to Chhatrapati Shahu Ji Maharaj University, Kanpur and is spread over 80 acres of land, on Kanpur-Agra-Delhi National Highway (NH -2), Bhauti, Kanpur. The campus is an excellent example of architectural marvel. Since inception, it is designed to train the extraordinary minds by providing the ecosystem for the holistic development. PSIT College of Higher Education has evolved a comprehensive student-centric learning approach consisting of several stages, designed to add significant value to the learners understanding in an integrated manner, covering relevant knowledge, practical skills and positive attitudes.
